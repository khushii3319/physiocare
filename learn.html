More About Physiotherapy

Healing through movement and care

What Does Physiotherapy Involve?

Physiotherapy uses physical techniques like guided exercises, massage, manual therapy, and posture correction to help people recover from injuries and illnesses. It promotes mobility, independence, and overall wellness.

Benefits of Physiotherapy:

  • Reduces or eliminates pain
  • Improves mobility and strength
  • Speeds up recovery post-injury or surgery
  • Manages chronic illnesses
  • Enhances posture and physical performance

Common Areas of Practice:

Physiotherapists work in areas like orthopedics, neurology, pediatrics, sports, and cardiopulmonary rehab. They help everyone from athletes to elderly patients live a better, more mobile life.
