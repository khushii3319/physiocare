Trust our professional physiotherapy center to have your back!

In today's fast-moving times with sedentary lifestyles, hours of slumping over a laptop or a hand-held device leads to chronic spine, postural and neck problems. And almost everyone has been a victim to the pain at some point or the other.

A few complications become more severe including Spinal Arthritis, Disc Bulge, Scoliosis, Sciatica, Spondylitis, and Degenerative Disc Disease. These need a proper diagnosis before moving on to healing therapy. When the pain starts to obstruct your daily life, it is time to see a physiotherapist.

Advanced Physiotherapy Clinic

At our Advanced Physiotherapy Clinic, we have over 7 years of trusted experience in diagnosing, treating, and guiding patients to overcome their chronic back problems. Most of the spine or musculoskeletal problems don't even need surgery (which is a common fear), but just a guided hand and regular conditioning.

We get to the root of your lumbar problems, including ergonomic triggers (environmental factors in living and working spaces), and start the therapy from the ground up. Based on our patient's treatment preference and the complexity of the problem we administer some of these therapies:

Our Specialized Treatment Methods

  • Chiropractic therapy - manual adjustment and/or manipulation of the spine for neuromuscular disorders.
  • Stretching, Core stability exercises
  • McKenzie exercise approach - designed to reposition any displaced intervertebral discs)
  • Pharmacological therapies - non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) are prescribed when necessary
  • Ergonomic advice - professionally assessing and adjusting your work and living environment
