Suffering from foot or ankle pain?

Our Foot Clinic helps you stand, walk, and live pain-free!

Whether it's plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinitis, flat feet, or a sprain, foot and ankle issues can make even basic movements uncomfortable. Left untreated, they can impact your posture, gait, and overall mobility.

We specialize in conditions like Heel Spurs, Shin Splints, Ankle Instability, and Arthritis-related foot pain. Our team ensures the right diagnosis and structured therapy to get you back on your feet — literally!

Expert Foot & Ankle Care

Our Foot Clinic offers over 7 years of dedicated service treating foot disorders with customized plans for both acute injuries and chronic conditions. Many of our patients recover without invasive interventions using guided rehab and consistent therapy.

We focus on gait correction, pressure points, biomechanical alignment, and strengthening. Based on the root cause of your foot or ankle issue, we tailor treatment protocols using a blend of proven methods:

Our Foot & Ankle Treatment Services

  • Manual mobilization - improving joint flexibility and reducing soft tissue stiffness.
  • Stretching and strengthening routines
  • Gait and balance training - correcting abnormal walking patterns and fall prevention.
  • Orthotic consultation - custom insoles and footwear support for structural issues.
  • Cold/Heat therapy & Taping - to reduce inflammation and support injured ligaments.
