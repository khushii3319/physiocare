In today’s lifestyle, repetitive strain, poor posture, and injuries can easily lead to shoulder problems such as rotator cuff issues, frozen shoulder, or tendinitis. Ignoring them only increases discomfort and limits movement.

Without timely intervention, shoulder disorders may worsen, leading to chronic pain or limited range of motion. Common conditions include Rotator Cuff Tears, Impingement Syndrome, Bursitis, and Arthritis. Effective therapy at the right time ensures long-term recovery and mobility restoration.
